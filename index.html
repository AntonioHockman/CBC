Mission Statement

We are open to the community FREE of charge but donations are welcome, throughout the years we have been a competive boxing gym taking our young boxers across the country and have grown proffesional fighters. We have also been a community center for the youth of Columbia, TN. We are here to help you achieve your goals, whether they be in the ring or in life. We train youth and adults to become high level competitors, but we also train people to become better versions of themselves. We are a family, and we welcome you to join us.


History

Founder

Dr. Morgan Hines Sr.

" Doc"

About:

Dr. Morgan Hines Sr. founded the Columbia Boxing Club out of his home in the 1990s, training local youths and producing many outsanding amateur and proffesional fighters throughout the years. He is a father, a mentor, a coach, and a talented artist. Morgan is also a local dentist in Columbia serving the community since the 1990s. He is married to Gwen Hines and commonly reffered to as "Doc" by his friends,family and boxing community.

Dr. Morgan Hines (far left), selected Mid-State Golden Gloves "Coach of the Year"

Jerome Pillow "J-one"

About:

Jerome Pillow was trained by Doc Hines since the age of 7, and was an outstanding amateur boxer with over 200 amateur fights and went on to be undefeated as a proffesional with a record of 16 victories. He is a father, a barber and long time trainer and mentor for the youth in Columbia.
